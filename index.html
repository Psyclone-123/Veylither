Vey's Writer's blocks

The Game

A WORK IN PROGRESS

Writer's block is my baby. It's a project that aims to make a fun, competitive word game that takes the fun from Scrabble and takes the pressure from other puzzle games to make an enjoyable experience for all!

I've always enjoyed playing word games with friends or family, but I've never liked how easy it was to cheat — whether that's researching words that can be used to win, or full-on overlays that can tell you the optimal word for the match at hand.

It's not done yet, and likely won't be done for a while, but when it is I'd love you guys to check it out on Google Play or the Apple Store.

The Mission

WHAT THIS GAME IS FOR

I want my game to be a safe space for players of all age groups to play together, and enjoy a good time. I want this game to be something that you could hand the oldest person in your family and have a game night!

Writer's block will be mostly free for everyone, preferably without ads — but I can't confirm it.

One last thing: I'm going to add so much content, achievements, and game modes that it'll have you entertained for hours on end!
